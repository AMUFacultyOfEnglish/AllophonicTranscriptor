Overview

This policy describes what data is collected whenever you visit this site and how that data is used.

Cookies

When you first visited this site, it placed cookies (small text files) in your browser’s cache. Each time you return to this site, your browser sends the cookies back. Enabling cookies in your browser is required for the best browsing experience. This site uses two cookies: _pk_id and _pk_ses. These are analytical cookies that define users as new or returning visitors and save the details of their visit, e.g. transcriptions they submitted. These are persistent cookies, i.e. they expire after a given period.

Website analytics

The two analytical cookies are a part of Piwik, an open source website analytics platform. Piwik collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as browser type, language preference, and the date and time of each visitor request. This data shows how visitors to this site use it. This data may inform decisions to improve user experience. This data may be released in aggregate (e.g. by publishing reports on trends in the usage of this site) or as a raw dataset. This data may be used for academic research. Piwik has been recommended by various privacy watchdogs for its strong privacy protection features, such as the anonymisation of IP addresses.
